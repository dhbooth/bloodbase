The Context

For generations, we have made incredible progress in stretching the limits of the human body. Dive deeper, fly higher, go faster, and live longer. You name it. And yet, there is so much progress to still be made.

The Problem

There are many who need blood, and more that are willing to donate it. Just in the wrong place at the wrong time. As a result, blood reserves end up in significant surplus in certain locations, while in other locations blood reserves are completely depleted.

The Team

The entire network of hospitals in the United States working together to effectively analyze, predict, and react to the inbalances of blood supply around the nation. Bloodbase allows hospitals to quickly communicate with each other to get blood where it needs to be as soon as possible.

The Solution

Put simply, the solution is bloodbase: a realtime depiction of blood trends by region throughout the United States. With this tool, doctors and hospitals everywhere will be able to effectively tend to those in critical condition.
